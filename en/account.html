Your Personal Data

Vibe collects and stores the following data related to your account:

Account Information

  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Account creation date
  • Login method

Profile

  • First name
  • Date of birth
  • Nationality
  • Profile photos
  • Biography
  • Interests

Activity

  • Created trips
  • Conversations
  • Sent messages
  • Contact requests

Technical Data

  • Last login
  • Device type
  • Location history
  • Connection logs

For more details on data collection and use, see our Privacy Policy.

Edit Your Information

You can edit most of your information directly in the app.

From the App

  • Profile photo: Profile > Edit > Photo
  • Personal information: Profile > Edit
  • Biography and interests: Profile > Edit
  • Email: Settings > Account > Email
  • Password: Settings > Account > Password

Non-editable Information

Some information cannot be edited directly:

  • Date of birth: Contact us if an error was made
  • Login method: Once chosen, it cannot be changed
Note: If you need to edit information not accessible in the app, contact us.

Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your Vibe account at any time.

Warning: Account deletion is irreversible. All your data will be permanently erased and cannot be recovered.

Method 1: From the App

  1. Open settings
    Tap your profile then the settings icon (gear)
  2. Go to Account section
    Select "Account" or "My account"
  3. Select "Delete my account"
    This option is usually at the bottom of the page
  4. Confirm your identity
    Enter your password to confirm
  5. Confirm deletion
    Read the information and confirm that you want to delete your account

Method 2: By Email

If you cannot access the app, you can request deletion by email:

  1. Go to the Contact page
    Click here to access the contact form
  2. Select "Account deletion"
    Choose this option from the dropdown menu
  3. Provide necessary information
    Enter the email associated with your account for verification
  4. Send the request
    We will process your request within a maximum of 30 days
Identity verification: To protect your account against fraudulent requests, we may ask you to verify your identity before proceeding with deletion.

Consequences of Deletion

Here's what happens when you delete your account:

Data Deleted Immediately

  • Your profile (name, photos, biography)
  • Your preferences and settings
  • Your published trips
  • Your pending contact requests
  • Your visibility on the map

Data Deleted Within 30 Days

  • Your messages in conversations
  • Your login data
  • Your activity logs
  • Your stored photos

Data Retained for Legal Reasons

Some data may be retained longer for legal reasons:

  • Reports: Reports you made or received may be kept for up to 3 years
  • Billing data: If you made purchases (kept according to legal requirements)
  • Anonymized data: Anonymous statistics may be retained

What You Lose

  • Access to all your conversations
  • Your connections with other travelers
  • Your trip history
  • All data associated with your account

Creating a New Account

After deletion, you can create a new account with the same email address. However:

  • No data from the old account will be recovered
  • You will need to recreate your profile from scratch
  • Your old connections will not be restored

Need Help?

If you have questions about managing your account or data, feel free to contact us.
