Quick Start

Welcome to Vibe! Here's how to get started with the app in a few simple steps.

  1. Download the app
    Vibe is available on the App Store (iOS) and Google Play Store (Android).
  2. Create your account
    Sign up with your email or connect via Google or Apple for quick access.
  3. Complete your profile
    Add your photos, nationality and travel interests so other travelers can get to know you.
  4. Explore the map
    Discover travelers present or coming to your destination.
  5. Connect
    Send contact requests and start chatting!

Creating Your Profile

A complete profile increases your chances of connecting with other travelers.

Essential Information

Profile Photo

Choose a clear photo where you are recognizable. Group photos or images without a face reduce trust from other users.

First Name

Use your real first name to facilitate authentic exchanges with other travelers.

Nationality

Indicate your country of origin. This helps other travelers find you and start conversations.

Date of Birth

Your age will be calculated and displayed to other users. Only people 18 and over can use the app.

Personalize Your Profile

  • Biography: Describe yourself in a few sentences. Talk about your passions, your favorite travel experiences.
  • Travel goals: Select what you're looking for (travel companions, local tips, activities, etc.)
  • Interests: Add your interests to find similar travelers.
  • Additional photos: Add up to 6 photos to show your personality.
Tip: A complete profile with multiple photos and a detailed biography receives up to 5 times more contact requests!

The Interactive Map

The map is the heart of Vibe. It allows you to visualize travelers from around the world.

Types of Pins on the Map

Green Pin

"Here Now" - The traveler is currently present at this location.

Blue Pin

"Coming Soon" - The traveler plans to go to this location soon.

Interacting with Pins

  1. Tap on a pin to see a profile preview
  2. Tap "View Profile" to access the full profile
  3. From the profile, you can send a contact request

Filtering the Map

Use filters to refine your search:

  • By city or destination
  • By travel dates
  • By presence type (here now / coming soon)

Creating a Trip

Share your travel plans so other travelers can find you.

Creation Steps

  1. Choose your destination
    Select the city you're going to. You can indicate if the destination is final or still being considered.
  2. Set the dates
    Enter your expected arrival and departure dates.
  3. Add details (optional)
    Estimated budget, planned activities, what you're looking for.
  4. Publish your trip
    Your trip will be visible on the map and in the Discover section.

Managing Your Trips

From the "My Trips" section, you can:

  • View all your active and past trips
  • Edit trip details
  • Delete a trip
  • See who's interested in your trip

Messaging

Messaging allows you to communicate with travelers you're connected with.

Starting a Conversation

  1. Visit a traveler's profile
  2. Tap "Send a message" or "Vibe"
  3. If the user accepts, you can exchange messages

Messaging Features

  • Text messages: Send instant messages
  • Read indicator: See when your messages are read
  • Notifications: Receive alerts for new messages
Safety reminder: Never share financial information or sensitive data via messaging.

Flash Meetups

Flash Meetups are spontaneous gatherings organized by travelers.

Creating a Flash Meetup

  1. Set the location
    Choose a public place for the meetup (cafe, square, monument, etc.)
  2. Choose the time
    Flash Meetups are for meetings in the next few hours.
  3. Add a description
    Describe the activity or purpose of the meeting.
  4. Set the number of participants
    Limit the number of people if necessary.

Joining a Flash Meetup

Available Flash Meetups appear on the map and in the Discover section. Tap on a meetup to see details and join.

Tip: Flash Meetups are ideal for meeting travelers quickly when you're already on location!

Safety and Blocking

Your safety is our priority. Here are the tools available to you.

Blocking a User

If a user is bothering you:

  1. Go to their profile or the conversation
  2. Tap the menu (three dots)
  3. Select "Block"
  4. Confirm the block

The blocked user will no longer be able to contact you or see your profile. This action is permanent.

Reporting a User

To report inappropriate behavior:

  1. Go to the profile or conversation
  2. Tap the menu (three dots)
  3. Select "Report"
  4. Choose the reason for reporting
  5. Add details if necessary

Safety Tips for Meetings

  • Always meet in public places
  • Tell someone you trust about your plans
  • Trust your instincts
  • Don't share sensitive personal information
  • Avoid getting into a stranger's vehicle

Settings

Customize your Vibe experience from settings.

Account

  • Edit profile: Update your information and photos
  • Change password: Secure your account
  • Email: Change your email address

Notifications

  • Messages: Enable/disable message notifications
  • Contact requests: Be alerted of new requests
  • Flash Meetups: Notifications for nearby meetups

Privacy

  • Profile visibility: Control who can see your profile
  • Location: Manage sharing your position
  • Data: Download or delete your data

Application

  • Language: Choose the interface language
  • Theme: Light or dark mode
