Our Commitment

Vibe is committed to maintaining a safe environment free from child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and any form of child exploitation. We have zero tolerance for any content or behavior that exploits, endangers, or sexualizes minors.

Zero Tolerance Policy: Any user found to be engaging in child exploitation, sharing CSAM, or attempting to groom minors will be immediately and permanently banned from the platform, and reported to the appropriate law enforcement authorities.

Age Requirement Policy

Vibe is designed exclusively for adults aged 18 years and older.

Minimum Age

Users must be at least 18 years old to create an account and use the application.

Age Verification

We collect date of birth during registration and verify that users meet the minimum age requirement.

Underage Account Removal

Any account found to belong to a person under 18 will be immediately suspended and deleted.

Profile Review

Our moderation team reviews profiles to identify and remove accounts that may belong to minors.

Prohibited Content and Behavior

The following content and behaviors are strictly prohibited on Vibe:

Prohibited Content

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) of any kind
  • Sexual or suggestive content involving minors
  • Content that sexualizes or objectifies children
  • Links to external sites containing CSAM or child exploitation material
  • Any imagery depicting child abuse or exploitation

Prohibited Behaviors

  • Grooming or attempting to establish inappropriate relationships with minors
  • Soliciting sexual content from or about minors
  • Sharing, requesting, or distributing CSAM
  • Discussing sexual acts involving minors
  • Attempting to meet minors for sexual purposes
  • Any form of child trafficking or exploitation
Important: Violation of these policies will result in immediate account termination and reporting to law enforcement authorities.

Detection and Prevention

We employ multiple measures to detect and prevent child exploitation on our platform:

Age Verification

Mandatory date of birth verification during account creation with automated checks.

Content Moderation

Review of user-generated content including profile photos and messages for policy violations.

User Reporting System

Easy-to-use in-app reporting tools for users to flag suspicious content or behavior.

Proactive Monitoring

Regular review of platform activity to identify and address potential risks.

How to Report

If you encounter any content or behavior that may involve child exploitation, please report it immediately:

In-App Reporting

  1. Tap the three dots (...) or report icon on the profile or message
  2. Select "Report"
  3. Choose "Child Safety Concern" or "Inappropriate Content Involving Minors"
  4. Provide any additional details that may help our investigation
  5. Submit the report

Email Reporting

You can also report child safety concerns directly to our dedicated email:

Email: contact@mcsedition.org
Subject Line: Child Safety Report - [Brief Description]

External Reporting

In addition to reporting to us, you may also report to:

  • USA: National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) CyberTipline
  • International: INHOPE - Network of hotlines for reporting illegal content
  • Local Law Enforcement: Contact your local police if you believe a child is in immediate danger

Our Response Process

When we receive a report related to child safety, we follow a strict response protocol:

Immediate Actions

  1. Content Removal: Suspected CSAM or exploitative content is immediately removed from the platform
  2. Account Suspension: The reported account is immediately suspended pending investigation
  3. Evidence Preservation: Relevant data is preserved for law enforcement

Investigation

  1. Our Trust & Safety team reviews all reports within 24 hours
  2. We assess the severity and nature of the violation
  3. We gather all relevant information for potential law enforcement reporting

Resolution

  • Confirmed Violations: Permanent ban and report to law enforcement
  • Underage Users: Account deletion and notification if appropriate
  • False Reports: Account reinstatement with documentation

Cooperation with Authorities

Vibe is committed to full cooperation with law enforcement agencies worldwide in the fight against child exploitation.

Legal Compliance

  • We comply with all applicable laws regarding the reporting of child sexual abuse material
  • We report suspected CSAM to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant authorities
  • We respond promptly to valid legal requests from law enforcement
  • We preserve evidence as required by law

Data Retention for Safety

In cases involving potential child exploitation:

  • We retain relevant data for a minimum period required by law
  • We provide data to law enforcement upon valid legal request
  • We maintain detailed records of reports and actions taken
Law Enforcement Contact: Law enforcement agencies can contact us at contact@mcsedition.org for urgent requests related to child safety matters.
